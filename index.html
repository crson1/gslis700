About Claire

Claire Larson is an aspiring library professional, currently enrolled in an MLS program at Queens College. She has several years of experience managing a small, community archive covering over a century's worth of history of a woman-led organization. Claire also has more than a decade's worth of experience working in digital communications and political organizing. Before beginning her communications career, Claire served in the United States Army as a Medical Laboratory Technician. Her varied background makes her adept at rising to any challenge.
